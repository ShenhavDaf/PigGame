Pig Game - How to play

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold":

  • If the player rolls a 1, he scores nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to his turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", he turns total is added to his final score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
